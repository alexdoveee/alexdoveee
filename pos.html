Wacoal Europe began their annual employee survey in 2018 and needed some branding and materials to support it. Their traditional corporate identity uses a simple black/white palette and Calibri font with no visual assets, so they needed some extra elements to stand out while still maintaining the monochrome colour scheme. With a strong emphasis on engagement and making Wacoal a better place to work, this campaign needed to express strong communication and the value of feedback from their employees. My idea was to add a communications-based logo and utilise imagery from around the Desborough office, to add some personality to the campaign that didn't overwhelm the minimal corporate feel.
