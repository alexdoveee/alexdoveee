A DOVE DESIGN
For the lingerie brand Elomi's 10th birthday in 2018, they hosted a party in central New York for a select few of their favourite bloggers and influencers. I was asked to create the graphics for the event, which ranged from simple vinyls and canvases to almost a hundred unique product tags, balloons and even a photo shoot area with backdrop and branded whiteboards to tie in to their successful 'Live Limitless' campaign. With the event taking place across the pond, it was a challenge to ensure the graphics were fit for purpose and were created at the right dimensions, but everything was printed without a hitch and the Elomi team sent across plenty of photos for me to see the graphics in action.
