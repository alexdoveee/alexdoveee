Xanadu Developments is a startup housebuilder based in Northamptonshire, aiming to introduce themselves to the market as a high quality, premium developer. Their logo needed to utilise the 'X' in their name, with a strict monochrome colour palette and reflecting a modern, minimalist aesthetic. All this while conveying the core brand values of quality and contemporary housebuilding. I produced five logo options each with their own unique corporate identities, reflected by the design of the stationary, sample brochure and banner.
