Harron Homes offer a multitude of optional extras to allow people to personalise their dream home as much as possible, and to the highest specification possible - named their 'Excellence' range. The brochure to advertise this range had not been updated since 2016, and its dated style no longer conveyed the luxury it was supposed to. So, my agency tasked me with re-styling the brochure, and also updating it to include a full catalogue of product lines. It was a long process that required a keen attention to detail, with product information needing to be precise and accurate, while also boasting a more luxurious look and feel in comparison to their existing marketing materials. I kept the basic Harron Homes branding; their colours, fonts, logos and graphical devices, but ensured it was a step more modern, upmarket and informative than its predecessor.
