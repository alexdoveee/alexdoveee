Housebuilder brochure design

Housebuilders offer a multitude of optional upgrades to allow people to personalise their dream home, and this catalogue needed to exude the luxury of the ranges on offer, while maintaining the utmost accuracy across the huge variety of product lines. With basic branding already in place, I ensured this new brochure was a step more modern, upmarket and informative than its predecessor.
